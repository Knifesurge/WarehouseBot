Warehouse Discord Bot
=========================

- Able to buy warehouses (Small, Medium, Large)
- Simple economy to be able to get money (Lotto, give command, etc)
- Able to buy crates (maybe a minigame to get them?)
- Can steal crates from other players
- Sell crates to get money
- Spend money to hire guards to protect your warehouse from being raided by another player
- Spend money to purchase weapons to be able to more effectively raid other players

For I/O functions:
- Should load all Profiles and needed classes at bot startup
- Should save in the event of a shutdown
- Could save in the event of an unexpected shutdown
- Could save periodically to assist with ^
